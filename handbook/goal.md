### Goal
**To maximize the LF**

If it's in the negative side and monotonic decreasing then maximizing LF means to leave this world.  
If it's in the positive side and monotonic increasing then maximizing LF means to live as much as more years as possible.

But deciding is not never this easy. There is always randomness, uncertainty, probabilities, chance, actions, time, and shitload of other factors thatn make it as hard as a complex 10,000 degree 10,000 order mathematical equation.
