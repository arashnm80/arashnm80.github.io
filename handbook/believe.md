we tend to choose to believe a set of rules, facts and principles for ourselves. because it will make everything much easier! there might be some good phones from xiaomi or htc or sony or ... but of course it's much easier to choose to believe **samsung is the best** and simply ignore everything else.

and another very important factor: consider a contest between 100 people with 10 winners. those who believe they are going to win, might win or not. but those who believe they are not going to win, will lose for sure. those who don't believe special in anything might win or might lose, but winning definitely will be harder for them cause they are not fanatic.

### negative beliefs
excuses, victim mentality, weak mentality, loser mentality, ...  
they work in the similar way by making everything easy for us. when we decide to believe these for ourselves it it's easy to get rid of guilty conscience of not trying, sit down and not fight, be a failure and be proud of it.
