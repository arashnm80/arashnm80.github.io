What does it mean to live?

Mathematically it means to have positive life factor.

In other terms it means to be free to choose whatever I whim and most importantly be able to enjoy it:
- sleep
- love and be loved
- sex
- music
- movie
- friends
- travel around the world
- workout
- eat
- buy
- pool
- work

Of course the perfect life almost never has been possible but I want good enough level of it.
