What does it mean to live?

Mathematically it means to have positive life factor.

In other terms it means to be healthy **(98%)**, wealthy **(20%)** free **(5%)** and have time **(5%)** to choose whatever I whim and most importantly be able to enjoy **(5%)** them:
- sleep **(50%)**
- lie down on grass **(98%)**
- partner to love and be loved **(0%)**
- Be my true self **(90%)**
- sex **(0%)**
- watch movies and series **(100%)**
- Listen to music **(100%)**
- spend time with family and friends **(5%)**
- travel around the world
- workout
- eat **(98%)**
- pool
- work in intriguing fields
- play **(100%)**
- dance

Of course the perfect life almost never has been possible but I want good enough level of it. Also health, wealth, freedom and time are prerequisites and necessary to give meanings to other items but don't mean anything on their own (like having DirectX and Steam before installing a video game).
