---
title: Life Factor
---

Integral of (Pleasure - Pain) throughout life.

If positive, it's a worthwhile life called *living*.  
If negetave, it's a wasted life called *only being alive* and would have been better to leave in the first place.

### Current situation
I know that where I am right now is negative LF and local optimum. So any change would involve hardship and pain which results more negative LF. After that depending on situation, parameters and my actions, I might reach a positive, zero, less negative or more negative number.
