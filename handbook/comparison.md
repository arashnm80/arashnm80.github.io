since everything is a choice and every choice is an exchange, one the best practices is to always think **instead of what?**

Instead of my current choice, what else can I do with the same time, energy, money, ...? are those other possible choices better than current one? if so, switch to them.

comparison should not be made by thinking about only one choice and thinking about how much is its success rate from 0 to 100. it should be between all options and comparing their cost, benefit, risk, ... . after all it's important to choose better over good and bad over worse.
