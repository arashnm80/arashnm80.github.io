> C'est la vie. Leave or [fight](../fight) for the [narrow possibility](../narrow-way-through) to live.

Maybe it's what I want, maybe it's what I have to do, maybe it's my choice, maybe it's law of the universe or maybe it's sth written into my source code by my genes, family and society. Whatever it is, it's the truth I can run from.

### fight for the narrow possibility to live
First of all it's a fight. trying to live means to be in a constant fight; to fight with depression and lack of dopamine, to fight with loss of loved ones, to fight with pain and loneliness, to fight with tendency to give myself up to sadness and pressure, to fight with stress and anxiety, to fight with cruel acts of this cruel world, to fight with idiots, frauds and oppressives, and most importantly to fight agaist addiction to fight and to see and seize the very small opportunities to live because the reason of fight is to get rid of it.

Secondly it's a narrow possibility. It's a possibility because I can't be certain of anything in the world, I can just calculate, estimate and play with probabilities. And it's narrow because for my personality and the world I live in, it's actually very hard to find a path with positive Life Factor.
