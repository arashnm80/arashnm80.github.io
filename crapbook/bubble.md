### limited view fallacy
every second that we lose by doing nothing infinite possibilities and chances are lost but we always define a limited bubble for ourselves and make it the metric for our happiness.

winner of grade n and n+1 leagues are very happy cause they are the 1st in their own bubble but everybody else is sad cause they are not. another example: consider our rating, score, income, ... is 50. if we believe we deserve 60 we always feel bad but if we see ourselves at 40 we feel happy all the time. so different self-concept leads to totally different feelings even if all the circumstances are the same.

when my parents remind me of my postponed tasks i get angry of them reminding me the harsh reality but the truth is that their reminder has no impact on reality.
